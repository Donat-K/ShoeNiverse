About Us

Sneaker Box company, was founded in 2017 out of a desire to provide the sneakerhead community with a singular source that served up all the relevant news in the footwear world. It has since evolved into the web’s premiere destination for sneakerheads-gathering release dates, previews of upcoming projects, NBA sneaker wears, and more in one premium channel. What’s more, we have strong connections with all of your favorite brands, meaning that Sneaker Box is constantly on the ground at exclusive media launches and product debuts bringing you inside access.

Sneaker Box also offers exclusive content through our Sneaker Box select channels, which showcases everything from community members’ sneaker collections to upcoming collaboration releases. And the content doesn’t end here on the homepage-we’re constantly delivering you extras via our various social media channels (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter).
